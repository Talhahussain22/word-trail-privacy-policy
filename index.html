Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how the app "WordTrail" collects, uses, and protects user information.

Information Collection and Use

The app does not require users to create an account and does not collect personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.

The app stores game-related data locally on the user’s device. This includes information such as:

  • Game progress and levels
  • Coins and rewards
  • Daily and classic word progress
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored only on the user's device and is not transmitted to any external server controlled by the developer.

Advertising

The app displays advertisements provided by third-party ad networks. These ad providers may collect certain information automatically, including device identifiers and usage data, in order to serve relevant advertisements.

The app does not have control over how third-party ad networks collect or use data. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those ad providers.

Children’s Information

This app is intended for users aged 13 years and above. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

Data Security

The app uses local storage on the device to store game data. Reasonable measures are taken to protect this information, but no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Consent
